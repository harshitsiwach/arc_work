# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Web3 freelancers, AI agent operators, and crypto-native gig workers using Arc Work — a decentralized freelance marketplace on the Arc blockchain. Users are tech-savvy, expect premium UI, and want to move fast between browsing gigs, managing escrows, bridging funds, and deploying agents. They're usually in a focused task flow.

## Product Purpose

Arc Work is an all-in-one onchain freelance marketplace where humans and AI agents can post gigs, find work, escrow USDC payments, bridge funds between chains, and build onchain reputation. It replaces Fiverr/Upwork with instant settlement and AI-powered work validation — no middleman, no waiting.

## Brand Personality

Precise, premium, professional. Think Stripe meets Linear with a touch of cyberpunk sophistication.

3 words: **Sharp. Trusted. Effortless.**

## Anti-references

- No SaaS-cream/card-heavy clone (no standard "startup template" look)
- No neon-on-black crypto cliché (not overdone Web3 aesthetic)
- No glassmorphism or gradient overload
- No heavy shadows or fake 3D
- No identical card grids repeating everywhere
- No side-stripe colored borders
- No gradient text

## Design Principles

1. **Speed is the feature.** Motion is purposeful — micro-transitions that guide the user, never choreography that delays them. 150-200ms on all transitions.
2. **Earned familiarity.** Users should feel at home immediately. Standard navigation patterns, predictable grids, consistent vocabulary. The tool disappears into the task.
3. **Trust through precision.** Tight spacing, aligned grids, crisp typography. Every pixel intentional. No half-baked states.
4. **Signal over decoration.** Color, motion, and emphasis reserved for states (active, hover, selected, loading, error). Nothing decorative.
5. **Minimalist depth.** Subtle elevation on cards and panels — think thin borders and soft shadows, not heavy layering.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum
- Supports reduced motion preference
- Dark mode by default with light mode support
